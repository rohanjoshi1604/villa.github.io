About us

GET INSPIRATION FOR YOUR NEXT TRIP

Dreaming about your next trip and then planning to make it happen could be a fantastic way to stay inspired during this time. Planning can be the most intimidating part about travelling so if you get that out of the way ahead of time you can save some admin and stress once the time comes to book your long-awaited trip knowing that all the details have been planned ahead of time & you are 100% ready to press GO!

TIME SPENT WITH FAMILY IS WORTH EVERY SECOND

From your destination list create a vision board, make a list on your computer, create a Pinterest board, whatever you do have at least 10 options Decide on how much you want to spend on your next trip Research when the best time is to visit your bucket list destinations Add in the average cost of each destination Based on your budget & the best time to visit your bucket list destinations you should be able to narrow down which trip makes sense to go on next.

YOU FOCUS ON THE FUN, LET US SORT OUT THE REST.

Make a bucket list of places you want to see – creating a bucket list of places you want to see is always a good way to narrow down where you really want to go. Use this list as a canvas of your “dream trips” – don’t hold back! If you aren’t sure about how to do this maybe think about previous trips as inspiration for what you like and don’t like. Perhaps you preferred a different type of meal or you enjoyed being indoors rather than outdoors.
